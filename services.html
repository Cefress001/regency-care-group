Services & Pricing

Premium care starts with a thoughtful investment.

Our Fee Structure

We operate as a Domestic Referral Agency (DRA), so you employ the caregiver directly while we provide concierge matching and support. Our fees are structured to reflect the depth of our service and to align our incentives with your family’s success.

  • Placement Fee: 18 % of the caregiver’s first year compensation (e.g., $75,000 salary × 18 % = $13,500). This fee covers our depth assessment, matching and replacement guarantee.
  • Monthly Retainer: Choose the tier of ongoing support that fits your needs. Retainers can be cancelled with 30‑day notice.

Placement fees are due within 30 days of placement start.

Why Invest?

Failed placements cost more than money—every turnover erodes trust, destabilizes your family and drains your time. Our pricing is anchored in the value of getting it right the first time. When you compare an 18 % placement fee and monthly retainer to the cost of three failed hires, the investment becomes clear. We’re here to save you time, reduce stress and protect your loved one’s dignity.
