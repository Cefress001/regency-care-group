Your Investment

Stability and trust aren’t expensive when you get it right the first time. We operate as a Domestic Referral Agency (DRA) so your caregiver is your employee, while we provide concierge matching and support.

Fee Structure

Our pricing is designed to align our incentives with your family’s success. You employ your caregiver directly and pay them their full wage. We charge a placement fee for the matching process and an optional monthly retainer for ongoing support.

  • Placement Fee: 18 % of the caregiver’s first year compensation (e.g., a $75,000 salary × 18 % = $13,500). This covers our consultation, match and 90‑day replacement guarantee.
  • Monthly Retainer: Choose the tier of ongoing support that fits your needs. Retainers can be cancelled with 30‑day notice.

Why Invest?

Failed placements cost more than money—every turnover erodes trust, destabilizes your family and drains your time. Our pricing is anchored in the value of getting it right the first time. By investing in a thoughtful match and ongoing stewardship, you protect your loved one’s dignity and your family’s peace.
