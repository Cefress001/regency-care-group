How Our Process Works

A thoughtful, four‑step approach to match, introduce and support your caregiver relationship.

Our Four‑Step Process

1

Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a calm conversation and a 90‑minute visit at your home. Beyond basics, we explore personalities, communication patterns, unspoken tensions and values. We listen for fears, hopes and unspoken boundaries to understand your family’s psychological terrain.

  • Comprehensive family dynamics assessment, including decision‑makers and household staff
  • Personality and attachment profiling
  • Discussion of medical and mobility needs
  • Listening for unexpressed expectations and boundaries
2

Curated Match

Within days, we present 2–3 candidates from our vetted network. Each caregiver has verified experience, glowing references and the relational capacity to thrive with your family. We select candidates whose nervous systems and communication styles complement yours.

  • Hand‑selected candidate presentation with profiles
  • Background checks and reference verification
  • Trial shift coordination and debrief
  • Replacement guarantee for the first 90 days
3

Introductions & Selection

We arrange introductions and trial shifts to observe chemistry in real time. You choose your caregiver with our counsel and guidance. We help navigate questions, expectations and boundaries so both parties feel clear and confident.

  • Scheduling and coordination of meetings
  • Support with interview questions and evaluation
  • Assistance finalizing your caregiver choice
  • Preparation for a smooth first day
4

Ongoing Support

We remain available after placement for check‑ins, conflict mediation and coaching. By staying engaged, we protect continuity, adjust care plans and sustain harmony over the long term.

  • Scheduled check‑ins with family and caregiver
  • Conflict mediation and relationship coaching
  • Care plan adjustments and concierge services
  • Access to additional training resources
